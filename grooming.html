Grooming

One-on-one low stress grooming session personalized to your dog based on his age, breed, personality, fears, or mobility limitations. Our patient, gentle groomer is recommended for skittish, senior, medically fragile, or injured dogs.

Comfort and confidence are the keys to a successful groom. We take the extra time to ensure your dog is comfortable with the process.

Despite efforts toward low-stress, your dog may benefit by splitting the grooming into two or more sessions. Good Karma is committed to do whatever we can to help your dog learn to accept and hopefully enjoy being groomed.

Only one dog groomed at a time

  • No dog is crated all day.
  • The groomer will notify you when your dog’s groom is completed and you are expected to pick up your dog within 20 minutes.
  • Prices vary according to breed and coat condition. Our expert groomer is happy to provide a competitive estimate at your request.
  • Purchase a discounted 5 trim pass and get your 6th trim for free!
  • Grooming available Tuesday through Saturday by appointment.
  • Call today to schedule a low-stress, gentle groom for your special fur baby. He’ll be happy you did!
