Q: What are the benefits of dog daycare?

A: There are many! Dogs are social animals and benefit from interaction with other dogs. At daycare they will learn to socialize with other dogs, get exercise and have fun. It will ease your mind knowing your dog is entertained in a safe place instead of being bored, frustrated or destructive at home while you are busy working.

Q: What are your hours?

We are open 7:00 am to 6:00 pm Monday through Friday, some Holidays and weekends depending on the needs of our clients. Drop off hours are from 7:00 to 9:00 am. Pickup is between 3:00 pm and 6:00 pm or other times by special notice to staff.
