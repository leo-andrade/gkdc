A tired dog is a well behaved dog.

Bring your furry family member to our daycare center for play, socialization, on-going good manners training and fun. We provide a safe, healthy environment with constant supervision, separate play areas for dogs of all sizes and an outdoor fenced area. We limit the number of dogs so your pet gets more one-on-one attention from our staff, who are certified in Pet First Aid through the American Red Cross.

To promote a healthy atmosphere we dim the lights and play soft music from 11:00 am to 1:30 pm to promote natural napping and relaxation time. This is also the time that grooming or exercise programs are run. Our facility boasts anti-fatigue rubber flooring which provides excellent traction and reduces stress on joints, backs and muscles. The flooring is also 100% recycled and causes no health or environmental concerns.

Hours and Rates

Our normal hours of operation are 7:00 am to 6:00 pm Monday through Friday.

Reservations are recommended as we allow a limited number of dogs to insure the highest staff-to-dog ratio. Please call to reserve space at least 24 hours in advance.

Daycare Guidelines

All dogs must meet these guidelines to insure the health and safety of the pets at Good Karma Dog Center

  • All dogs must be 12 weeks of age or older
  • All dogs 7 months or older must be spayed or neutered to attend daycare
  • All dogs must have up-to-date vaccinations. Owners must submit written proof from their veterinarian of DHPP and Bordetella vaccinations. Rabies is required for puppies and dogs over 4 months of age. Bordetella vaccines are required every 6 months.
  • All dogs must be in good health. Owners will certify that their dog(s) are in good health and have not been ill with a communicable condition in the last 30 days. Upon admission, all dogs must be free of any condition that could potentially jeopardize other dogs, including fleas or ticks. Dogs that have been ill with a communicable condition in the last 30 days will require veterinarian certification of health to be admitted or readmitted.
  • All dogs must be non-aggressive and not food or toy protective. Owners will certify their dogs have not harmed or shown any aggressive or threatening behavior towards any person or dogs. The safety and health of all the dogs at Good Karma Dog Center is our concern.
  • All dogs must have a complete up-to-date Application on file to attend daycare.
  • All dogs must undergo a temperament test performed on the first day your dog attends daycare. Cost for temperament testing is $20.
  • Good Karma Dog Center reserves the right to dismiss any dog from daycare, boarding or training should the dog show aggressive or threatening behavior at any time.
